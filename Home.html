Environmental Sustainability

The responsible interaction with the environment to avoid the depletion or degradation of natural resources, ensuring long-term ecological balance. It involves adopting practices that protect ecosystems, reduce pollution, conserve water and energy, and minimize waste. The goal of environmental sustainability is to meet the needs of the present without compromising the ability of future generations to meet their own needs, fostering a harmonious relationship between human activities and the natural world.
